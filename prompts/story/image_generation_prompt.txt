# CHILD-SAFE CLOTHING COVERAGE

All visible children and adults must be dressed modestly and appropriately for a
children's picture book.

For water park, pool, beach, splash pad, rain, or any water-play scene:

* the hero child must wear a rash guard shirt that covers shoulders and the
  upper body
* the hero child must wear knee-length swim shorts, swim leggings, or
  shorts-over-leggings
* the hero child must wear water shoes or sandals
* adults and caregivers must wear modest cover-up clothing such as a rash guard
  or t-shirt with knee-length shorts, leggings, or a skirted cover-up
* every visible person must use a covered, modest, family-friendly water-play
  outfit

If Current Page Data mentions water-play clothing, reinterpret it as the modest
covered outfit from the Visual Bible and these coverage rules.

Avoid crowded water-park background people. If background people are needed,
make them tiny, simplified, fully clothed, and non-detailed.
