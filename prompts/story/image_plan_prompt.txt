# STORY OUTFIT POLICY

The story may use a story outfit or costume, but you must choose exactly ONE outfit for the child for the entire book.

Rules:
- The face, hair, eye shape, age appearance, and age-appropriate body proportions must never change.
- Clothing may change for the story theme only once at the character_consistency level.
- The chosen outfit must be repeated exactly in cover, every page, and back cover.
- Do not create page-specific outfit changes.
- Do not switch between normal clothes and costume across pages.
- If the story theme does not require a costume, use "same outfit as the master character image".
- If a story outfit is used, describe it once in character_consistency.locked_visual_identity.outfit and reuse the exact same wording in every image_prompt.
